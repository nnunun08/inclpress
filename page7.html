Order Overview

File Upload Instructions

To upload your print files, click on your order and use the Upload Files button in the order details.
Alternatively, you can upload the files to Google Drive and share the link with us at serviceincpress.com. Please make sure to include your project name when sharing the files.

Proof Review Instructions

After you upload your files, we’ll check them for any issues. If we find anything that needs fixing, we’ll contact you by email. If your files are ready for print, a proof file will be uploaded to your Order Details for you to review. Please check the proof carefully and click “Approve” to confirm.
Printing will begin only after payment is completed.
