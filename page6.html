File Upload Instructions

You can upload your print files in one of the following ways:

  • 1. Upload the files to Google Drive and share access with us at serviceincpress.com,
    Please include your project name in the message when sharing, so we can identify your order.
    or
  • 2. After completing your order, go to My Account → Order Details and use the Upload Files button.

After You Place an Order

  • 1. Please prepare and upload your print files as soon as possible, regardless of payment status.
  • 2. Once we receive your files, we will review them. If there are any issues that could affect printing, we will notify you by email.
  • 3. If the files are ready for print, we will upload a proof file to your order page under My Account → Order Details for your review.
  • 4. Please carefully review the proof files. You must click “Confirm” on each proof to approve them before we proceed with printing.

Note: Printing will only begin after payment is completed through the invoice we send. So please make sure to complete payment and approve your proof files in a timely manner.
